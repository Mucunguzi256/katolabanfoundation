Our Story
Our Story

Built for Isingiro's Communities

Kato Laban Foundation was established with a clear vision: to empower communities in Isingiro District by providing knowledge and sustainable development that enhances a quality life for the most vulnerable populations.

Operating from Kikagate Town Council in Isingiro District, we have dedicated ourselves to improving health outcomes, expanding access to quality education, and promoting climate education and climate justice.
